    May 5, 2019

About Me

Hi, my name is Corey Chen, I am currently dedicated to C# development.
Usually in the company is mainly responsible for the development of WinForm, and sometimes the development of WEB and Report.
I believe that on the road of development, only continuous learning can have continuous driving force.
So I want to use this blog to record problems encountered during development and learn new technologies.
Please feel free to contact me if you have any comments.

關於我

嗨，我的名字是 Corey Chen，我目前致力於 C# 開發。
通常在公司主要負責 WinForm 的開發，有時還會開發 WEB 和 Report。
我相信，在開發的道路上，只有不斷學習才能有持續的動力。
所以我想用這個部落格記錄開發過程中遇到的問題並學習新技術。
如果您有任何意見，請隨時與我聯繫。
